Alternative / Specializes / Decorator / Interceptor

Toggle in beans.xml:

  • <alternatives> – switch between the default and your @Alternative bean
  • <specializes> – choose which implementation “wins”
  • <interceptors> – turn logging on/off
  • <decorators> – wrap / unwrap your decorator
